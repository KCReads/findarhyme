Hi, I’m Kathryn — online as KC Reads.

This project started because I wanted storytime planning to feel easier and less time-consuming.

As a children’s librarian, I’ve spent plenty of time digging through notes and bookmarks trying to find the right song or rhyme. I wanted a simple way to search and use what I needed.

I have my MLIS and a background in early childhood education and have worked in libraries for about 10 years.

This isn’t meant to tell you how to run your storytime. It’s here to give you options and save time.
